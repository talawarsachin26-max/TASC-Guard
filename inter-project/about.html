About Our Project

In today's digital world, privacy has become one of the most important concerns for individuals and organizations alike. Whether working in a library, classroom, office, café, airport, or any public environment, people frequently access sensitive information such as passwords, banking details, personal messages, confidential documents, and business data on their screens.

However, many users remain unaware that others around them may be able to view this information, intentionally or unintentionally. This common but often overlooked threat, known as shoulder surfing, can lead to privacy breaches, unauthorized access to information, and potential security risks.

Our mission is to empower users with intelligent privacy protection by utilizing Artificial Intelligence to detect potential shoulder-surfing threats and safeguard sensitive information. We aim to create awareness about digital privacy, promote secure technology usage, and provide users with confidence while working in public and shared environments.

Our Vision

Our project was developed with the vision of creating a smarter and more proactive approach to digital privacy protection. By leveraging Artificial Intelligence and computer vision technologies, the system continuously monitors the user's surroundings through a webcam and detects the presence of individuals near or behind the user.

When a potential privacy threat is identified, the system instantly alerts the user and can take protective actions to help prevent sensitive information from being exposed. Rather than relying solely on physical privacy screens or user awareness, our solution introduces an intelligent layer of security.

Important Disclaimer

While this system serves as an intelligent privacy assistant, users should remember that no automated solution can completely replace personal awareness and responsible digital practices.

Environmental conditions such as lighting, camera quality, viewing angles, and user positioning may influence detection performance. Therefore, users are encouraged to remain attentive to their surroundings and continue following established cybersecurity best practices.

Our project is intended to complement user vigilance, providing an additional layer of protection against privacy threats and helping create a safer, more privacy-conscious digital world.
